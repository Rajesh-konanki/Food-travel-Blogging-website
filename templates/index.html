💡 Travel Tips

Before You Go

🗺️

Research Local Dishes

Know the 3 signature dishes of every destination before you arrive. Ask locals, not Google.

🌅

Eat Where Locals Eat

If the menu has photos and is in 6 languages, walk away. Find the place with no English menu.

💰

Budget for Food

Allocate 40% of your travel budget to food. The best meals are always worth the splurge.

📸

Photograph Everything

But eat first. A cold plate photographed is a meal wasted. Priorities matter.
